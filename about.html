About AIP

AIP Capital is a global alternative investment firm specializing in real assets, private credit, and strategic partnerships across multiple sectors.

Pre-Eminent Dedicated Asset-Based Finance Investor

AIP Capital ("AIP") is a multi-strategy investment manager focused on opportunities in asset-based finance.

  • AIP believes its unique investment strategy, relationships, and hands-on approach enable it to execute its mission of generating attractive risk-adjusted returns for its clients across market cycles.

  • With offices in Stamford, New York City, Dublin, Tokyo, Seoul and Singapore, AIP maintains a global footprint and is backed by a full platform of over 50 professionals across origination, finance, technical, legal, risk management and underwriting

Our Values

At AIP Capital, our culture is the foundation of everything we do. We believe that long-term success is built on clarity of purpose, disciplined execution, and the strength of the relationships we cultivate. These values guide our decisions, shape our partnerships, and define how we operate as a firm.

We are Committed

We are a tightly knit team dedicated to delivering exceptional outcomes and building a resilient, high-performing business. Our commitment extends to our shareholders, customers, investors, and partners—ensuring we approach every opportunity with the focus, integrity, and determination needed to achieve their long-term objectives. We take ownership of our work and hold ourselves accountable to the highest standards.
