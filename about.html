About AIP

AIP Capital is a global alternative investment firm specializing in real assets, private credit, and strategic partnerships across multiple sectors.

AIP Capital Vision

To grow together with our partners by delivering thoughtful capital, operational insight and a commitment to responsible, long-term performance.

Pre-Eminent Dedicated Asset-Based Finance Investor

AIP Capital ("AIP") is a multi-strategy investment manager focused on opportunities in asset-based finance.

  • AIP believes its unique investment strategy, relationships, and hands-on approach enable it to execute its mission of generating attractive risk-adjusted returns for its clients across market cycles.

  • With offices in Stamford, New York City, Dublin, Tokyo, Seoul and Singapore, AIP maintains a global footprint and is backed by a full platform of over 50 professionals across origination, finance, technical, legal, risk management and underwriting (1)

  • AIP wholly owns AIP Capital Advisors which is an SEC registered investment advisor with $5Bn of total platform AUM (2)
