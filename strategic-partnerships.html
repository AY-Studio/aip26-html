Our Partnerships & Investments

BUSINESS AVIATION

Sankaty Jet Capital

Business aviation lending platform focused on providing financing solutions for counterparties across the business aviation industry.

  1. Direct Lending: Providing secured financing solutions for the acquisition and refinancing of business jets to high-net-worth, ultra-high-net-worth and corporate clients.
  2. Fleet Operators: Providing secured financing solutions for branded charter and scheduled Part 135 business jet operators.
  3. Inventory / Broker-Dealer Financing: Providing inventory financing lines to IADA-accredited aircraft dealers and brokers.

EQUIPMENT FINANCE

Harbor Point Equipment Finance

An equipment finance platform focused on acquiring seasoned performing and under-performing equipment lease and loan portfolios and originating equipment leases and loans and syndicating as well as servicing lease/loan portfolios.

  1. Portfolio Acquisitions
  2. Lease & Loan Origination
  3. Lease Servicing

BeYoke Capital

AIP Capital and BeYoke have formed a strategic partnership to originate aircraft and engine investment opportunities for Japanese investors.

The partnership will originate aircraft and engine investment opportunities for Japanese investors via Japanese Operating Lease (JOL), Japanese Operating Lease with Call Option (JOLCO) structures, and secured loan products.

Learn More

AIP Capital – ASIA

AIP Capital formed a joint-venture partnership to pursue strategic investments in the aviation investment markets of Korea, Asia, and the broader Asia-Pacific region.

This joint-venture partnership is focused on leveraging AIP's asset expertise and global capabilities, with regional relationships and experience to originate bespoke investment opportunities in aviation for APAC-based investors.
