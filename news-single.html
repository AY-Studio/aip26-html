Phoenix Aviation Capital and AIP Capital Announce Closing of Inaugural $550 Million Senior Unsecured Notes Offering

Posted on: 15th Jan, 2026 at 9:30am

Phoenix Aviation Capital Limited ("Phoenix"), a leading global aircraft leasing platform, and AIP Capital Partners LLC ("AIP Capital"), a global alternative investment firm specializing in real assets and private credit, today announced the successful closing of Phoenix's inaugural senior unsecured notes offering.

The transaction consisted of $550 million aggregate principal amount of 5.875% Senior Notes due 2029, which were issued at par. The Notes were offered and sold to qualified institutional buyers in the United States pursuant to Rule 144A under the Securities Act of 1933, as amended (the "Securities Act"), and to certain persons in offshore transactions outside the United States in reliance on Regulation S under the Securities Act.

Strategic Milestone

This landmark transaction represents a significant milestone for Phoenix and demonstrates the company's strong market position and access to diverse funding sources. The proceeds from the offering will be used to:

  • Refinance existing indebtedness
  • Fund aircraft acquisitions
  • Support general corporate purposes
  • Enhance financial flexibility for future growth

"This successful debut in the unsecured debt capital markets is a testament to Phoenix's differentiated business model and strong execution track record. The transaction provides Phoenix with enhanced financial flexibility to capitalize on attractive opportunities in the aircraft leasing market."

— John Smith, CEO, Phoenix Aviation Capital

Transaction Highlights

The offering demonstrates several key strengths of Phoenix's platform:

  1. Market Access: Successful entry into the unsecured debt capital markets
  2. Pricing Power: Notes issued at par with competitive pricing
  3. Investor Demand: Strong institutional investor interest
  4. Strategic Flexibility: Diversified funding sources for growth initiatives

Financial Impact

The proceeds will strengthen Phoenix's balance sheet and provide additional liquidity for strategic initiatives. The company expects the transaction to be immediately accretive to earnings and enhance its competitive position in the global aircraft leasing market.

The aviation leasing sector continues to demonstrate robust fundamentals, with strong demand from airlines seeking modern, fuel-efficient aircraft. Phoenix's platform is well-positioned to capitalize on these market dynamics through its flexible capital structure and deep industry relationships.

Management expects the proceeds from this offering to support the company's growth trajectory over the next several years. The transaction also establishes Phoenix as a recognized issuer in the capital markets, opening up additional financing alternatives for future growth initiatives.

About Phoenix Aviation Capital

Phoenix Aviation Capital is a leading global aircraft leasing platform focused on providing innovative financing solutions to airlines worldwide. With a portfolio of modern, fuel-efficient aircraft, Phoenix serves a diverse customer base across multiple geographies and is committed to supporting the sustainable growth of the aviation industry.

Key facts about Phoenix:

  • Global presence across major aviation markets
  • Portfolio of modern, fuel-efficient aircraft
  • Strong relationships with leading airlines
  • Commitment to sustainable aviation growth

About AIP Capital Partners

AIP Capital Partners is a global alternative investment firm specializing in real assets and private credit. With approximately $6.2 billion in assets under management, AIP Capital provides flexible capital solutions across aviation, infrastructure, equipment finance, and other asset-backed sectors. The firm maintains offices in Stamford, New York, Dublin, Singapore, Seoul, and Tokyo.
