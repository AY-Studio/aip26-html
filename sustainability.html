Sustainability

Building a sustainable future through responsible investing and ESG integration.

Our Commitment to ESG

At AIP Capital, we believe that integrating environmental, social, and governance (ESG) considerations into our investment process is essential for long-term value creation.

Our ESG framework guides our decision-making, helping us identify risks and opportunities while creating positive impact for investors, portfolio companies, and society.

Our ESG Pillars

AIP Capital integrates ESG considerations into its investment underwriting and ongoing monitoring to better assess long-term risk, protect asset value and support durable financial performance. ESG is embedded in the same disciplined, value-driven framework that underpins our investment philosophy.

Environmental

We actively work to reduce our environmental footprint and support portfolio companies in implementing sustainable practices. From reducing carbon emissions in our aviation assets to promoting energy efficiency across our investments, environmental responsibility is central to our strategy.

Social

We prioritize the well-being of our employees, partners, and the communities where we operate. Through fair labor practices, diversity and inclusion initiatives, and community engagement, we strive to create positive social impact across our entire value chain.

Governance

Strong governance frameworks are fundamental to our operations. We maintain the highest standards of business ethics, transparency, and accountability. Our governance practices ensure responsible decision-making and effective oversight across all aspects of our business.
