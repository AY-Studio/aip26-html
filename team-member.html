Jared Ailstock

Managing Partner

LinkedIn Profile

Jared Ailstock is a Managing Partner at AIP Capital with over 15 years of experience in aviation finance and alternative investments. He has been instrumental in building the firm's aviation asset management platform and has overseen more than $2 billion in aircraft transactions.

Prior to co-founding AIP Capital, Jared held senior positions at leading financial institutions where he specialized in structured finance and asset-backed securities. His expertise spans aircraft valuations, lease negotiations, and portfolio management across commercial and cargo aviation sectors.

Jared holds an MBA from the Wharton School at the University of Pennsylvania and a Bachelor's degree in Finance from the University of Michigan. He is a frequent speaker at aviation finance conferences and serves on the board of several industry associations.
