Stamford, CT

Senior Investment Analyst

About the Role

We're seeking an experienced investment professional to join our real assets team. You'll be responsible for sourcing, analyzing, and executing investment opportunities while working closely with portfolio companies to drive value creation.

This role offers significant exposure to complex transactions across aviation, infrastructure, and other real asset sectors. You'll work alongside senior partners and contribute to strategic decision-making at the highest levels of the firm.

Key Responsibilities

  • Source and evaluate investment opportunities in the real assets space
  • Conduct comprehensive financial analysis and due diligence
  • Build detailed financial models to support investment decisions
  • Prepare investment memoranda and presentations for investment committee
  • Work with portfolio companies to implement value creation initiatives
  • Monitor portfolio company performance and provide ongoing support
  • Collaborate with internal teams across origination, legal, and operations

Qualifications

  • 5-7 years of experience in investment banking, private equity, or related field
  • Strong financial modeling and analytical skills
  • Excellent written and verbal communication abilities
  • Bachelor's degree in Finance, Economics, or related field; MBA preferred
  • Deep understanding of real assets and infrastructure investments
  • Ability to work independently and manage multiple projects simultaneously
  • Strong attention to detail and commitment to excellence

What We Offer

At AIP Capital, you'll work alongside industry-leading professionals on complex, high-impact transactions that shape the future of alternative investing. We foster a culture of collaboration, innovation, and continuous learning, where talented individuals are empowered to grow their careers and make meaningful contributions.

Our distributed ownership model ensures that team members share in the success they help create, aligning interests and driving exceptional performance.
