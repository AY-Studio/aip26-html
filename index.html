Our Strategies

Real Assets

Acquiring and servicing commercial aircraft and engines to drive attractive risk-adjusted returns, investing via various co-investment, fund vehicles and other structures.

Investing across asset types and age including:

  1. Next Generation Aircraft
  2. Mid-Life Aircraft
  3. Opportunistic End-Of-Life Aircraft
  4. Aircraft Engines

Phoenix Aviation Capital: A full-service aircraft lessor exclusively managed by AIP Capital. AIP Capital manages Phoenix's global $3bn fleet consisting of 66x aircraft including a 737 MAX 8 orderbook with Boeing.

Find out more

Private Credit

Direct origination with airlines providing bespoke credit solutions including:

  1. Secured Aircraft and Engine Financing: Providing bespoke credit solutions with aircraft and engines (across asset type and age) as collateral.
  2. Esoteric Assets: Providing bespoke credit solutions with varying asset collateral types
    1. Spare Parts: Spare engines and parts
    2. Other Hard Assets: Financing engine stands, ground-service equipment, hangers
    3. Soft Collateral: Financing Slots, Gates & Routes, Loyalty Programs, Intellectual Property, Forward Flow Receivables
  3. Pre-Delivery Payment Financing: Financing pre-delivery payments for airlines acquiring new aircraft.

Asset Management

Witt Lake Asset Management (WLAM) is an asset management firm focused on providing investment management services to investment funds, separately managed accounts and other vehicles managed by WLAM. WLAM provides advisory services to privately offered investment funds, single investor funds, co-investment vehicles, pooled investment vehicles and other similar types of investment entities focused on sourcing, buying, trading, and managing investments and securities, on both a discretionary and non-discretionary basis.

WLAM currently has approximately $1.5 Billion of assets under management with the majority of these assets being aviation private credit instruments managed on behalf of insurance clients.

Strategic Partnerships & Control Investments

  1. Business Aviation (Sankaty Jet Capital): Business aviation lending platform focused on providing financing solutions for counterparties across the business aviation industry.
