AIP Capital is a global multi-strategy investment manager focused on opportunities in asset-based finance. AlP, together with its affiliates, manages approximately $6 billion of assets on behalf of a diversified global investor base.

Pre-Eminent Dedicated Asset-Based Finance Investor

AIP Capital ("AIP") is a multi-strategy investment manager focused on opportunities in asset-based finance. Through a differentiated investment strategy, deep industry relationships, and a hands-on approach, AIP seeks to generate attractive risk-adjusted returns for its clients across market cycles.

Headquartered in Stamford with offices in New York City, Dublin, Tokyo, Seoul, and Singapore, AIP maintains a strong global footprint. The firm is supported by a fully integrated platform of more than 50 professionals spanning origination, finance, technical, legal, risk management, and underwriting, and wholly owns AIP Capital.

Our Strategies

Investing in tangible assets with lasting value

Our real assets strategy targets investments in infrastructure, real estate, and natural resources. We focus on assets that provide stable cash flows and inflation protection while contributing to sustainable development.

With a disciplined approach and extensive operational expertise, we create value through active asset management and strategic improvements.

Flexible credit solutions for complex situations

Our credit platform provides customized financing solutions across the capital structure. We leverage deep industry expertise and relationships to deliver innovative structures that meet our partners' unique needs.

From senior secured loans to structured credit products, we offer flexible capital solutions that create value for all stakeholders.

Strategic partnerships driving exceptional outcomes

We build long-term partnerships with high-quality businesses and management teams. Our control investments focus on companies where we can add significant value through operational expertise and strategic guidance.

By combining capital with deep industry knowledge and a collaborative approach, we help our partners achieve sustainable growth and create lasting value.

Active management delivering superior returns

Our asset management approach combines rigorous fundamental analysis with active portfolio management. We focus on identifying undervalued opportunities and implementing strategies that maximize risk-adjusted returns.

Through continuous monitoring and proactive management, we adapt to changing market conditions and optimize portfolio performance across all investment strategies.
